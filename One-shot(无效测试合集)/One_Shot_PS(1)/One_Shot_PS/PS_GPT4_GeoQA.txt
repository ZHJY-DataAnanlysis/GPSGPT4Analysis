answer:In the given diagram, AB is parallel to CD, and EF is perpendicular to AB at point E. Also, EF intersects CD at F, and it is given that angle 1 is equal to 60°.

According to the properties of parallel lines and the angles formed when a transversal crosses them, the alternate interior angles are equal. In this case, angle 1 and angle 2 are alternate interior angles.

Since angle 1 is 60°, angle 2 will also be 60° because of the alternate interior angles theorem.

∠2 = 60°
